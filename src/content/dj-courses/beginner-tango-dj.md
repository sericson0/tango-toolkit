Welcome to the Beginner Tango DJ course! This series will walk you through everything you need to know to start DJing at milongas.

## What You'll Learn

- **Music selection** — Understanding tango, vals, and milonga
- **Tanda structure** — How to build tandas that dancers love
- **Cortina** — The role of the cortina between tandas
- **Flow** — Creating energy and variety throughout the night

## Course Structure

This course contains multiple lessons. Work through them in order for the best experience. Each lesson includes text, videos, and practical exercises.
